WINGuard Icon Concepts

Three directions before replacing any production asset: a shield-led safety mark, a wing/fader hybrid, and a record-first console mark. All three use the current WINGuard tone: guarded, studio-facing, and slightly warm rather than cold-tech.

Concept A: Shield + Console

The safest, most literal option. It says protection, routing, and recording at a glance.

Concept B: Wing + Faders

More product-like and modern. It feels closer to a control surface and less like generic security software.

Concept C: Record Core

More compact and app-icon friendly. The record dot is dominant, with just enough console geometry to anchor it.

Concept D: Behringer-Inspired Shield

Same guarded shape as concept A, but pushed into a yellow/black palette that feels closer to Behringer hardware branding.

Concept E: Soundcheck Play Core

Focuses on playback and soundcheck, using the yellow/black base with a stronger transport symbol in the middle.

Concept F: Winged Record Guard

More energetic and emblem-like, combining a guarded record dot with wing-like lines and fader bars.
